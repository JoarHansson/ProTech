Allow us to share the journey of how we came to be
In the scenic city of Gothenburg, Sweden, Protech emerged as a visionary company dedicated to enhancing the outdoor experience. Founded by outdoor enthusiasts, their mission was to create innovative, eco-friendly technology for nature lovers. Their flagship product, the TrailBlazer ProPhone, became a global sensation, offering features like advanced GPS, weather alerts, and long-lasting battery life. As Protech's reputation grew, they expanded their product range, focusing on quality, sustainability, and community engagement. From their Gothenburg headquarters, Protech continues to lead the way in developing next-gen outdoor tech, making outdoor adventures safer and more enjoyable worldwide.
